How to use

- Click a node to highlight its neighborhood

- Double-click a node to rename it (saved in browser)

- Drag nodes to rearrange, scroll to zoom, drag background to pan

- Gold nodes: received PAL in the last 7 days | Blue nodes: no recent activity

- Node size: proportional to PAL balance held

- Thicker / orange edges = larger token transfers
